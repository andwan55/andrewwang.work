SUCCESS KOACH COUNSELING SITE
❮ ❯
August 2022 - November 2022

Success Koach College Counseling is a college application counseling program and service provided by Harpal Kochar. He requested a website redesign for its site structure, brand look, and logo. I revised the website's navigation to be more intuitive and chose a new color palette that better suited the business to implement in the new site layout. Additionally, I designed graphics assets in Figma to be used across pages, including infographics as well as service icons.
