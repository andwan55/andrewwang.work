About Me

Hi there, my name is Andrew Wang! I graduated in 2023 from Stony Brook University with a major in Technological Systems Management and a specialization in Computer Science.
I grew up in Fremont, California and have a passion for game and graphic design as well as art. I enjoy working with colors and designing interfaces. Much of my experience has to do with computer science-related projects as well since I took several courses on them in university. For a more detailed list of my skills, see my resume below.
In my free time, I like to learn new recipes, play tennis & badminton, and chip away at my video game library.
